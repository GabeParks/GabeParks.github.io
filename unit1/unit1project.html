Celestial Apparitions

Celestial Apparitions is a classic rock/alternative cover band who excels at emotional ballads as well as driving tunes.
This band has been around since the winter of 2019, where it nearly fell apart if it wasn't for the three founding members rallying together and becoming closer. Of those founding members, only the guitarist Gabe Parks remains.

Members

  • Madison Parks - Lead Singer
  • Adam Hutchings - Lead Guitarist
  • Gabe Parks - Rhythym Guitarist
  • Kael Alexander - Rhythym Guitarist
  • Caleb Bolen - Drummer

    • Each of these players have provided an important aspect to this group. Madi has provided a captivating voice that pulls on the heart's strings. Caleb provides a consistency of excellence as he pounds away at his drums, letting fill after fill speak for his skill. As for Adam, Gabe, and Kael; The three guitarists add a good blend of sound, helping support each musician with some sort of key fill.
